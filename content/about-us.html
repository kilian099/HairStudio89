ABOUT US

The beginning of us

Transform Your Look, Transform Your Life: Visit Our Hair Studio Today!

Hairstudio89 is a team of passionate hair professionals who have come together to create a unique and unforgettable hair experience for our clients.

Our journey began during the COVID pandemic when our co-founders, Araceli and Daniel, decided to take their passion for hair and create a studio where they could provide exceptional service and build genuine relationships with their clients.

At HairStudio89, we understand that your hair is an extension of your personal style and identity. That's why we specialize in creating custom Hair looks that are tailored to your unique features and preferences. Our skilled stylists are passionate about their craft and are committed to staying up to date with the latest trends and techniques to ensure that you receive the highest quality service.

Be Bold, Be Beautiful: Let Our Salon Experts Bring Your Vision to Life!

At HairStudio89, we believe that every client deserves personalized attention and care. That's why we offer one-on-one consultations and custom-tailored services to ensure that your experience with us is as unique as you are.

We pride ourselves on using only the best and most natural products, providing our clients with an experience that not only leaves them looking great but feeling great too. We believe in building a strong relationship with our clients, and we are committed to creating a welcoming and relaxing environment where they can feel comfortable and at ease. Thank you for choosing Hairstudio89, and we look forward to working with you to create the perfect hair look for you!
